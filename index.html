Love Coding

At Love Coding, we believe the future will be decided by well-rounded technical entrepreneurs. To that end, we promise to :

  1. Create and curate technical tutorials around cutting-edge technologies (most notably machine learning and blockchain).
  2. Explore innovative real-life case studies of entrepreneurs and technologists building incredible things, so you can explore what they did and draw inspiration and creativity from it.
  3. Give you the all the other resources you need, from financial savvy to knowledge of visa laws, so that you can live your fullest life as a builder of meaningful, socially impactful, and new technologies.

About Us

A publication that focuses on helping developers practice their skills no matter what level of their career they're at: from beginners looking to get their first job without a degree, to people looking to tackle more advanced topics and tutorials. We do tutorials and longer courses on technical topics. We also do news items that show how free and open source technologies are being used and impacting real people.
